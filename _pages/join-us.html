## Join CARISET At the **Center for Advanced Research in Intelligent Systems and Emerging Technologies (CARISET)**, we are building a team of passionate researchers to advance the frontiers of **Artificial Intelligence, Distributed Systems, Hardware Security (PUFs), and Quantum Computing**. If you are motivated, curious, and eager to contribute to cutting-edge research, we encourage you to apply for one of our open positions. --- ## Open Positions ### Postdoctoral Researcher **1 position, starting Fall 2026** CARISET is seeking a highly motivated postdoctoral researcher to work on **hardware-based security (PUFs), AI-driven security frameworks, and AI + Quantum integration**. **Areas of expertise (preferred):** - Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning - Distributed & Secure Computing - Hardware-based Security and PUFs - Quantum Computing & Quantum AI **Application:** Interested candidates should email **Dr. Frederic Rizk** at [frizk@uttyler.edu](mailto:frizk@uttyler.edu) with the following: - Curriculum Vitae (CV) - Brief statement of research interests - Contact information for three references --- ### Graduate Research Assistants **2 positions, starting Fall 2025** We are recruiting two graduate research assistants to join the group starting Fall 2025. Current and prospective graduate students are welcome to apply. **Research areas include:** - AI and Deep Learning - Distributed and Cloud Computing - Quantum Computing & Integration with AI - Cybersecurity & Privacy **Requirements:** - Strong background in computer science, computer engineering, or related field - Experience in programming (Python, C/C++, or similar) is preferred but not required **Application:** Current and prospective students should contact **Dr. Frederic Rizk** at [frizk@uttyler.edu](mailto:frizk@uttyler.edu) with a CV and transcript to discuss opportunities. Prospective students should also apply to the **Computer Science or Electrical Engineering graduate programs** through the [UT Tyler Graduate School application portal](https://www.uttyler.edu/admissions-aid/graduate/admission-requirements). --- ### Undergraduate Researchers **Open Positions** We welcome motivated undergraduate students from all levels and backgrounds. Whether you are exploring research for the first time or preparing for graduate school, CARISET provides hands-on opportunities to learn and contribute. **Opportunities include:** - Undergraduate research projects (CS/ENGR 4315 or equivalent) - Thesis and capstone projects - Summer research internships No prior research experience is required. If you’re interested, contact **Dr. Frederic Rizk** at [frizk@uttyler.edu](mailto:frizk@uttyler.edu). --- ## Why Join CARISET? - Collaborate on **cutting-edge projects** at the intersection of AI, security, and quantum computing - Gain **hands-on experience** with advanced computing and security systems - Work in a supportive, interdisciplinary research environment - Opportunities to **publish in top venues** and present at international conferences
