Meet the Team

Principal Investigator

Dr. Frederic Rizk
Assistant Professor, Electrical and Computer Engineering
Director, CARISET Lab
The University of Texas at Tyler

Dr. Rizk leads the Center for Advanced Research in Intelligent Systems and Emerging Technologies (CARISET) at UT Tyler. His research lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence, hardware security, and emerging technologies, with a focus on AI-powered hardware-software co-design, physical unclonable functions (PUFs), and deep learning systems. He holds M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Engineering from the University of Louisiana at Lafayette, and a Bachelor of Engineering from Notre Dame University, graduating Summa Cum Laude. He has served as a research scientist and postdoctoral fellow, contributing to high-impact projects across academia and industry. Dr. Rizk is a recipient of the prestigious UT System Rising STARs Award and is a member of IEEE, ACM, and the Order of the Engineer.
