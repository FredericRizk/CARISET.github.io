Principal Investigator

Dr. Frederic Rizk
Assistant Professor, Electrical and Computer Engineering
Director, CARISET Lab
The University of Texas at Tyler

  • Director of the Center for Advanced Research in Intelligent Systems and Emerging Technologies (CARISET).
  • Research focus: AI, hardware security, physical unclonable functions (PUFs), deep learning, and hardware-software co-design.
  • Holds Ph.D. and M.S. degrees in Computer Engineering from the University of Louisiana at Lafayette.
  • Earned B.E. in Computer and Communication Engineering from Notre Dame University, graduating Summa Cum Laude.
  • Previously served as a research scientist and postdoctoral fellow at the Informatics Research Institute, University of Louisiana at Lafayette.
  • Recipient of the UT System Rising STARs Award.
  • Member of IEEE, ACM, and Phi Kappa Phi.
