**Course Description:**

Taught EENG 4311 Signals and Systems, focusing on theoretical and applied methods in signal processing and linear systems analysis.

Topics include:
- Signal and system modeling and properties  
- Convolution and linear time-invariant (LTI) systems  
- Fourier series and Fourier transform analysis  
- Laplace transform techniques and applications  
- Z-transform and discrete-time systems  
- Sampling theory, aliasing, and spectral analysis  
- Discrete Fourier Transform (DFT) and MATLAB-based implementation  

Students developed analytical and computational skills for modeling and solving engineering problems in signal processing and systems.

**Prerequisites:**
EENG 3305 Linear Circuit Analysis II, EENG 3308 Programming Languages for Design

**Tools Used:**
MATLAB
