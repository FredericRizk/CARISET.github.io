**Course Description:**

Taught EENG 4311 Signals and Systems, focusing on theoretical and applied methods in signal processing and linear systems analysis.

Topics include:
- Signal and system modeling  
- Time-domain analysis and convolution  
- Fourier series and Fourier transform techniques  
- Laplace transform methods  
- Z-transform and discrete-time system analysis  
- Sampling and reconstruction concepts  
- Frequency-domain analysis and MATLAB-based simulations  

Students gained experience in analyzing engineering systems in both continuous and discrete domains.

**Prerequisites:**
EENG 3305 Linear Circuit Analysis II, EENG 3308 Programming Languages for Design

**Tools Used:**
MATLAB
